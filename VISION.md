# Vision

Business OS is not an ERP.

Business OS is a collection of trusted Business Assistants designed for small
businesses.

The mission is to remove operational complexity so owners can spend more time
serving customers, growing their business, and caring for their teams.

## Current Assistant

Employee Assistant: TimeTrack Pro

TimeTrack Pro helps workers and business owners record work time, calculate
payroll-related income, track allowances and expenses, and prepare HR-ready
reports.

## Product Direction

Every Assistant must provide value on its own and integrate into the larger
Business OS platform.

Customers should enable only the Assistants they need.

No Assistant should force another Assistant.

## Success

Success is when a small business owner says:

"This software makes running my business easier."
