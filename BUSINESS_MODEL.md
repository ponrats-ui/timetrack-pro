# Business Model

Business OS is designed as a modular SaaS platform.

Customers subscribe only to the Assistants they need. Every Assistant should
function independently and integrate seamlessly with the Shared Core.

## Product Packaging

Business OS is a family of Assistants:

- Employee Assistant
- Payroll Assistant
- POS Assistant
- Inventory Assistant
- Finance Assistant
- Tax Assistant
- CRM Assistant
- Business Assistant
- Document Assistant
- Analytics Assistant
- AI Assistant

## Independence

Every Assistant must be independently useful and independently sellable.

No Assistant should force adoption of another Assistant for its core value.

## Platform Integration

Assistants should share platform capabilities for identity, database,
reporting, notifications, configuration, themes, localization, and AI.

## Product Boundary

Business OS should avoid ERP-style monolith architecture.

The business model depends on trust, clarity, modularity, and customer choice,
not forced complexity.
