# AI Strategy

AI in Business OS exists to help owners understand and act.

AI should:

- Explain.
- Summarize.
- Recommend.
- Warn.
- Forecast.

AI should not replace business responsibility. It should support decisions by
making information clearer.

## Rules

- Never fabricate facts.
- Never guess financial facts.
- Never hide uncertainty.
- Always explain recommendations.
- Always distinguish estimates from certainty.
- Always rely on user-provided data and verified rules for financial or tax
  guidance.

## Examples

Inventory Assistant:

"Rice stock may run out in two days based on recent usage."

Tax Assistant:

"You may be approaching a VAT threshold. This is an estimate based on recorded
sales and should be reviewed with an accountant."

Business Assistant:

"This week's sales are lower than your recent average. The largest drop was on
weekday afternoons."

## AI Product Standard

If AI cannot explain why, it should not recommend.
