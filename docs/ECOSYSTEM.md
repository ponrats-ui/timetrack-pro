# Business OS Ecosystem

Business OS should cooperate with the business ecosystem Thai SMEs already use.

It should not behave like a closed system.

## Ecosystem Partners

Business OS should work with:

- Excel
- CSV
- PDF
- Email
- Accounting software
- Government systems
- Bank exports
- APIs
- Future Business OS packages

## Assistant Ecosystem

Business OS grows through focused assistants:

- Employee Assistant
- Inventory Assistant
- POS Assistant
- Tax Assistant
- Finance Assistant
- CRM Assistant
- AI Business Assistant
- Founder Dashboard

Each assistant solves one problem and can connect to the rest of the platform
when that connection helps the customer.

## Customer Ecosystem

Our customers include restaurants, cafes, street food sellers, small factories,
transport companies, shops, repair stores, family businesses, and small
offices.

These businesses already have workflows. Business OS should make those
workflows safer, clearer, and easier.

## Ecosystem Rule

Cooperate before competing.

Connect before replacing.

Simplify before expanding.
