# Monetization

Business OS is designed as modular SaaS.

Customers should subscribe only to the Assistants they need. The business model
should respect customer choice and avoid forcing unnecessary complexity.

## Potential Revenue Streams

The following are strategic possibilities, not commitments:

- Assistant subscriptions.
- Premium analytics.
- Cloud synchronization.
- Partner integrations.
- API access.
- Marketplace.
- Enterprise deployment.
- Consulting.
- Training.

## Monetization Principles

- Keep basic value understandable.
- Do not hide simple workflows behind unnecessary complexity.
- Align pricing with value delivered.
- Let customers adopt Assistants gradually.
- Protect trust over short-term revenue extraction.
