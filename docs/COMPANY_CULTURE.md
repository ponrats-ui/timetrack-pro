# Company Culture

Technology exists to reduce stress, not create it.

Business OS culture is built around respect, simplicity, clarity, and trust.

## We Respect Users

Our users are busy running businesses.

They may be serving customers, managing staff, driving, cooking, repairing,
selling, or handling paperwork while using the product.

The product should respect their time and attention.

## We Simplify Work

We do not celebrate complexity.

We simplify workflows until the next step is obvious.

Simple wins when it helps a customer finish real work.

## We Explain Calculations

Customers should understand how totals, reports, imports, exports, payroll
summaries, and AI suggestions are produced.

Transparency builds trust.

## We Build Trust

Trust matters more than feature count.

A feature that customers cannot understand or verify is not finished.

## We Build Workflows

We build workflows, not feature lists.

The goal is to help customers move from problem to completed work with less
stress.

## We Stay Small Business First

Business OS is for Thai SMEs first.

Restaurants, cafes, street food sellers, small factories, transport companies,
shops, repair stores, family businesses, and small offices should feel seen in
the product.
