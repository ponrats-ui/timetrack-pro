# Day One

Business OS should always behave like a Day One company.

Day One means staying close to customers, keeping decisions simple, and
remaining willing to redesign workflows when the product becomes harder than
the problem it solves.

## What Day One Means Here

- Listen to small business owners before adding complexity.
- Prefer clarity over completeness.
- Ship only when trust is protected.
- Keep business rules configurable.
- Avoid monolithic architecture.
- Keep AI explainable.
- Make every workflow easier to understand than the manual process it replaces.

## Product Standard

If a feature requires training before a common workflow can be used, redesign
the workflow.

If a calculation cannot be explained, do not present it as certain.

If a release does not save time, improve accuracy, increase confidence, improve
visibility, or reduce stress, it should not ship.
