# Customer Journey

Business OS is designed for small business owners who want practical help
without adopting a complex ERP system.

## First Contact

The owner should understand the product quickly:

- Business OS is a platform of Business Assistants.
- Each Assistant solves one operational problem.
- TimeTrack Pro is the Employee Assistant.

## First Value

The first workflow should create value within minutes.

For TimeTrack Pro, that value is recording work time, seeing payroll-ready
calculations, and understanding the month through reports and analytics.

## Growing Usage

As the business grows, the owner may add more Assistants:

- POS Assistant for sales.
- Inventory Assistant for stock visibility.
- Finance Assistant for money movement.
- Tax Assistant for preparation and estimates.
- Business Assistant for plain-language insights.

## Trust Moments

The product must be especially careful when handling:

- Payroll
- Taxes
- Employee records
- Reports
- Exports
- AI recommendations

These moments must be explainable and predictable.
