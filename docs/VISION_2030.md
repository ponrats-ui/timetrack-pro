# Vision 2030

Every small business deserves enterprise-quality software that feels simple.

Business OS will become the easiest business platform for Thai SMEs.

## What This Means

By 2030, Business OS should help Thai SMEs manage work, people, inventory,
sales, tax, finance, customers, and decisions through focused assistants.

Each assistant should solve one real business problem.

Each assistant should work with the tools customers already use.

Each assistant should reduce stress instead of adding operational burden.

## Who We Serve

Business OS is built for restaurants, cafes, street food sellers, small
factories, transport companies, shops, repair stores, family businesses, and
small offices.

We build for people who are busy running businesses, not learning software.

## Platform Direction

Business OS should be:

- Small Business First.
- Open by Default.
- Thai-first in language and workflow.
- Modular by assistant.
- Transparent in calculations and AI.
- Friendly to Excel, CSV, PDF, JSON, email, accounting software, government
  systems, bank exports, and APIs.

## Success

A Thai SME should be able to start with one assistant, get value quickly, and
grow into the rest of Business OS only when the business is ready.
