# Vision 2030

Business OS exists to become the easiest software platform for Thai SMEs.

## Vision

Every small business deserves enterprise-quality software that feels simple.

## What 2030 Should Look Like

By 2030, Business OS should help Thai small businesses manage work, people,
inventory, sales, tax, finance, customers, and decisions through focused
Assistants.

Each Assistant should solve one job clearly.

Each Assistant should work with the tools customers already use.

Each Assistant should reduce stress instead of adding new operational burden.

## Platform Direction

Business OS should become:

- Small Business First.
- Open by default.
- Thai-first in language and workflow.
- Modular by Assistant.
- Transparent in calculations and AI.
- Friendly to Excel, CSV, PDF, JSON, email, accounting software, government
  systems, and APIs.

## Success

A Thai SME should be able to start with one Assistant, get value quickly, and
grow into the rest of Business OS only when the business is ready.
