# Founder Story

Business OS was founded by Ponrat Saripan.

The work started with a practical operational problem: helping people record
work time, calculate payroll-ready income, track allowances and expenses, and
prepare reports. That first product became TimeTrack Pro, the Employee
Assistant.

While building TimeTrack Pro, the larger pattern became clear. Attendance was
only one part of the daily stress small businesses carry. Owners also deal with
sales, stock, money movement, taxes, documents, employees, and reporting. Many
of those workflows are connected, but existing software often presents them as
complex systems that require training.

Business OS grew from that realization.

The founder's role is to protect the product vision, business architecture,
customer philosophy, AI governance, and long-term platform strategy. Engineering
decisions must support the product vision. Implementation convenience should
not override clarity, trust, or maintainability.

The founding principle is simple: build software that helps owners finish real
work with less stress.
