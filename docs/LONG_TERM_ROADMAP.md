# Long-Term Roadmap

This roadmap describes the intended direction of Business OS. It may change as
customer feedback reveals better sequencing.

## Today

Employee Assistant: TimeTrack Pro

Focus: attendance, work records, payroll-ready calculations, reports, exports,
analytics, and production quality.

## Next Platform Directions

POS Assistant

Inventory Assistant

Finance Assistant

Tax Assistant

CRM Assistant

AI Assistant

Business Intelligence

Platform APIs

Marketplace

Partner Ecosystem

## Roadmap Principle

Business OS should grow from real operational pain. Each Assistant must solve a
clear customer problem and remain independently valuable.
