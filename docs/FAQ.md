# FAQ

## What is Business OS?

Business OS is a platform of Business Assistants for small businesses.

## Is Business OS an ERP?

No. Business OS is not ERP. It is designed to avoid ERP-style complexity by
offering focused Assistants that solve specific business problems.

## What is TimeTrack Pro?

TimeTrack Pro is the current product and the first Assistant in Business OS. It
is the Employee Assistant for attendance, payroll-ready calculations, reports,
calendar visibility, analytics, and exports.

## Who is Business OS for?

Business OS is for small businesses, especially owner-operated businesses and
teams that need simple operational tools without enterprise complexity.

## Why Thai first?

Language and local business habits matter. Thai-first UX helps the product fit
the first target market while preserving a platform architecture that can expand
later.

## Does Business OS replace accountants?

No. Business OS should help owners understand, prepare, estimate, and plan. It
should make conversations with accountants easier, not replace professional
advice.

## What role does AI play?

AI assists by explaining, summarizing, recommending, warning, and forecasting.
AI must not fabricate facts, guess financial information, or hide uncertainty.

## How does Business OS make money?

The intended model is modular SaaS. Customers subscribe only to the Assistants
they need. Other possible future directions are documented in
[Monetization](MONETIZATION.md).
