# Values

Business OS is built around calm, durable values.

- Simple over complex.
- Reliable over clever.
- Helpful over fancy.
- Trust over feature count.
- Customer problems over technical curiosity.
- Long-term thinking over short-term hacks.
- Configurability over hardcoding.
- Maintainability over speed.
- Explain before impress.
- Human first.

## How Values Apply

Product decisions should reduce stress for real owners.

Engineering decisions should protect long-term maintainability.

AI decisions should preserve transparency and explainability.

Business decisions should earn trust before optimizing revenue.
