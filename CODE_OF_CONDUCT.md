# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are building trusted business assistants for small businesses. Everyone
working on this project is expected to act with respect, patience, and care.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Assume good intent.
- Give feedback that is specific and helpful.
- Protect user trust.
- Respect the founder's product vision and project governance.
- Prioritize customer problems over personal preference.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Sharing private or confidential information without permission.
- Intentionally misleading users, contributors, or maintainers.
- Ignoring project governance after being reminded.

## Product Responsibility

Business OS handles sensitive operational information, including money,
employees, payroll, inventory, and taxes.

Contributors must never fake calculations, hide important information, or
encourage decisions without explaining why.

## Enforcement

The repository owner and founder may remove access, reject contributions, or
take other appropriate action to protect the project, users, and contributors.
