# Long-Term Roadmap

Business OS is not an ERP.

Business OS is a platform of business assistants for Thai SMEs.

Each assistant solves one real business problem.

## Current Assistant

Employee Assistant: TimeTrack Pro

Focus: employee time, work records, payroll-ready summaries, HR export/import,
open data handoff, and production quality.

## Future Assistants

- Employee Assistant
- Inventory Assistant
- POS Assistant
- Tax Assistant
- Finance Assistant
- CRM Assistant
- AI Business Assistant
- Founder Dashboard

## Roadmap Rule

Each assistant must solve one problem before it expands.

User feedback beats assumptions. The roadmap may change when real customer
problems reveal a better sequence.
