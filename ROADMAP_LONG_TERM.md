# Long-Term Roadmap

Business OS is a modular SaaS platform made of independent Business Assistants.

## Phase 1

Employee Assistant: TimeTrack Pro

Focus: work records, payroll-ready calculations, allowances, expenses,
dashboard visibility, HR reports, backup and restore, and production quality.

## Phase 2

POS Assistant

Focus: simple sales capture, receipts, daily sales visibility, and integration
with inventory and finance workflows.

## Phase 3

Inventory Assistant

Focus: stock visibility, low-stock warnings, simple purchasing workflows, and
owner-friendly inventory insights.

## Phase 4

Finance Assistant

Focus: money coming in, money going out, cash visibility, simple expense
tracking, and business summaries.

## Phase 5

Tax Assistant

Focus: tax estimates, deadline reminders, threshold warnings, document
preparation, and clear explanations before professional advice is needed.

## Phase 6

Business Assistant

Focus: plain-language business insights across Assistants, trend detection, and
explainable recommendations.

## Future Assistants

## Roadmap Rule

User feedback beats assumptions. The roadmap may change when real customer
problems reveal a better sequence.
