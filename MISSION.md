# Mission

Business OS exists to help small business owners spend less time managing
software and more time growing their business.

We do not build software.

We build confidence.

We reduce stress.

We save time.

## Who We Serve

Our customers are restaurants, cafes, street food sellers, small factories,
transport companies, shops, repair stores, family businesses, and small
offices.

We build software for people who are busy running businesses, not learning
software.

## What This Means

Business OS should help customers:

- Finish daily work faster.
- Reduce mistakes.
- Understand calculations.
- Share information with people they already work with.
- Keep using the tools that already help them.
- Feel more confident before making decisions.

## Current Mission Focus

TimeTrack Pro is the Employee Assistant inside Business OS.

It is the first assistant because employee time and payroll-ready records are a
daily pain point for many Thai SMEs.
