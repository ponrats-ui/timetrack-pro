## Vision

Business OS exists to make running a small business simple.

We are not building traditional ERP software. We are building trusted Business
Assistants that help owners save time, reduce mistakes, understand their
business, and reduce operational stress.

## Product Philosophy

Technology should serve business owners.

Software should explain.

Software should reduce stress.

Software should be understandable without training.

Every feature must solve a real business problem.

## Founder Principles

- Customer problems come before technical elegance.
- Simplicity beats complexity.
- Trust beats feature count.
- Configurability beats hardcoding.
- Long-term maintainability beats shortcuts.
- User feedback drives the roadmap.
